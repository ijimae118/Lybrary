Apple Watch.

All new. For a better you.

Apple Watch

$399

Display: LTPO AMOLED, 1.78 inches

OS: watchOS 5.0

CPU: Apple S4

RAM: n/a

Internal Memory: 16 GB

SIM: Electronic SIM card (eSIM)

Battery: Up to 18 h (mixed usage)

Description

All-New Design.

The largest Apple Watch display yet. New electrical heart sensor. Re-engineered Digital Crown with haptic feedback. Entirely familiar, yet completely new, Apple Watch Series 4 resets the standard for what a watch can be.

A better experience all the way around.

Curved corners precisely match the radius of the case, creating a more pleasing, continuous appearance. Narrower borders enable a viewing area that’s over 30% larger, while a new display technology called LTPO improves power efficiency.

Proactive Heart Monitor.

Low and high heart rate notifications. Fall detection and Emergency SOS. New Breathe watch faces. It’s designed to improve your health every day and powerful enough to help protect it.

ECG on your wrist. Anytime, anywhere.1p

Apple Watch Series 4 is capable of generating an ECG similar to a single-lead electrocardiogram. It’s a momentous achievement for a wearable device that can provide critical real-time data for doctors and peace of mind for you.

Ultimate workout partner.

More than just a sports watch. Automatic workout detection. New yoga and hiking workouts. Advanced features for runners like cadence and pace alerts. See up to 5 metrics at a glance as you precisely track all your favorite ways to train.

Sweat, surf, and swim proof.2

Apple Watch Series 4 is water resistant to 50 meters and tracks both pool and open-water workouts. Turn the Digital Crown to eject water from the speaker using a burst of sound.

Comprehensive Activity Tracker.

New head-to-head competitions. Activity sharing with friends. Personalized coaching. Monthly challenges. Achievement awards. Get all the motivation you need to close your Activity rings every day.

Track your health data in one convenient place.

All your heart data, as well as information from your health and fitness apps, is collected in your iPhone Health app. Activity, sleep, mindfulness and nutrition are highlighted there in one snapshot.

The freedom of cellular.

Walkie-Talkie, phone calls and messages. Stream Apple Music and Apple Podcasts.3 New ways to use Siri. Built-in cellular lets you do it all on your watch — even while you’re away from your phone.4

All-new S4 chip.

The S4 is more than just a processor. It’s a complete Silicon in Package (SiP), with the entire system fabricated onto a single component, allowing Apple Watch to pack amazing capabilities into a tiny space.

50 percent louder speaker.

Bigger and even more powerful, the Apple Watch Series 4 speaker provides dramatically improved audio quality for Siri and Walkie-Talkie.
