Description

Multitask without boundaries

Adapted to your busy life at home and on the go, the refined Galaxy Tab S4 is your ultimate entertainment and multitasking assistant.

Samsung DeX effortless productivity

Get the productivity of a computer with the mobility of a tablet. Samsung DeX is available on a Samsung tablet for the first time with Galaxy Tab S4. Enable DeX without any accessories necessary or simply add a keyboard, mouse or monitor for even more efficiency.

Ready to work

Put on your creative, artistic, productive or work hat. The Galaxy Tab S4 works like a desktop with a task bar, multi-window capability and familiar gestures like drag & drop with Samsung DeX. When you want to have a desktop-like experience, just launch Samsung DeX via the on-screen Quick Panel and add a compatible keyboard and mouse for further convenience.

Work your way

Release your ideas faster, with the setup you want. That’s where Samsung DeX with external monitor comes in. You can keep using the Android user interface on the tablet, with Samsung DeX on a larger screen. Or watch the monitor and use the tablet as a touch pad, digitizer, or touch keyboard.

Lose yourself in entertainment

Galaxy Tab S4 provides an expansive viewing area on a compact device. The vivid 10.5” screen size was made possible with minimised bezels and slimmer profile. Enjoy a more immersive viewing experience when watching your favourite content with the bright sAMOLED and incredibly detailed 2560 x 1600 display.

Surround yourself in the sound

Quad Speakers tuned by AKG deliver true-to-life sound. Now you can experience auto rotating stereo output, mastered by the experts at AKG. And Dolby Atmos® contributes moving audio technology, enabling 3D surround sound with an added overhead dimension that brings moments to life.

Binge watch for hours on end

Galaxy Tab S4 has a battery capacity of 7,300mAh lasting for up to 16 hours of video playback*, so enjoy back to back episodes of your favourite shows. And if your battery does run out, bring it to a full charge in just 200 minutes with fast charging.
