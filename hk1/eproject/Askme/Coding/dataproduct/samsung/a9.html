4x fun with the world's first 4x camera.

Samsung Galaxy A9

Description

The world’s first quad camera smartphone

The Galaxy A9 features a quad camera - the first of its kind on a smartphone. Capture every moment in all its natural glory with the intelligent camera system, which automatically selects the optimum settings for the conditions. Snap whenever inspiration strikes.

Colourful design that defines you

The sleek and distinctive design of the Galaxy A9 means it’s easy to grip, while making a bold fashion statement. The premium 3D rear glass casing comes in Caviar Black, Lemonade Blue, and Bubblegum Pink, seamlessly blending form and function.

Shoot with confidence with quad camera

The Galaxy A9 is the world’s first smartphone to offer an Ultra-Wide Camera, Telephoto Camera, 24MP Main Camera, and a Depth Camera, which works with the Main Camera to let you manually adjust the depth of field in your images. It also features Scene Optimiser and Flaw Detection, so you can start shooting superior photos straight away.

Infinite viewing pleasure

See a supremely vivid world on your screen. The Galaxy A9 features a 6.3” FHD+ Super AMOLED Infinity Display that produces sharp contrast and dynamic colours for a more immersive viewing experience.

Always on when you need it

Stay up to speed with essential updated and important information. The Galaxy A9 features an Always On Display, which lets you view key information like the date, weather, time and incoming notifications in a flash on its wide screen.

Experience cutting-edge performance

The Galaxy A9 houses a powerful and highly responsive mobile Octa-Core processor with 6GB RAM. It gives you uninterrupted high performance to get more things done throughout the day.

Store more without deleting

Take more of your life with you. The Galaxy A9 provides 128GB of internal memory, so you can store your photos, movies, and files for easy access. If you need more space, you can add up to 512GB of expandable storage with a microSD card.

Enjoy more, charge less

The Galaxy A9 comes with a fast-charging 3,800 mAh (typical) battery, so you can spend less time plugged in and more time out and about. Plus, thanks to its USB-C port, you can plug cables in either way, for added convenience.

Convenient mobile security

Personalised protection

Now you can unlock your phone instantly, by simply holding it to your face. Face Recognition technology grants access to registered users only, to prevent personal information falling into the wrong hands. With Samsung Knox, this solution also safeguards your data and apps.

Protection at your fingertips

Simplify access with security you can trust. The Galaxy A9 features reliable fingerprint scanning, enabling you to login to websites and apps easily, for seamless multitasking.
